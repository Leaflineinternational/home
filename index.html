Privacy Policy

Effective date: 06 September 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Leafline International Private Limited ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our services, including our integrations with Login with Amazon (LwA) and the Amazon Advertising API.

Who we are

Legal entity: Leafline International Pvt. Ltd.
Contact: info@leafline.co.in

Information we collect

  • Account & Contact: name, email, company, role (when you contact us or sign up).
  • Operational data from Amazon Ads: profile IDs, campaign/ad group/keyword entities, performance metrics (impressions, clicks, spend, sales, ACOS/ROAS), and settings (bids, budgets, negatives) that you authorize.
  • Technical data: IP, device/browser, and basic logs used for security and troubleshooting.

How we use information

  • To provide and improve campaign management features (e.g., launching campaigns, adjusting bids, adding negatives).

  • To troubleshoot, secure our services, and comply with applicable laws and Amazon policies.

Login with Amazon (LwA) & Amazon Ads API

We request only the scopes required for Amazon Ads. You can revoke access at any time from your Amazon account. Access tokens expire; we store refresh tokens securely to maintain your connection, if you choose to link your account.
  • Lawful basis: performance of a contract and legitimate interests in providing the service.
  • Data retention: operational data is retained as long as your account is active or as required by law; you can request deletion.
